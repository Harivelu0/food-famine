Famine Astro

Famine Astro is a sophisticated application designed to address the critical issue of insufficient crop production in India. Its primary function revolves around analyzing an array of crucial factors to predict and anticipate potential crop shortages. By harnessing advanced analytical techniques and leveraging vast datasets, Famine Astro provides invaluable insights into the agricultural landscape, helping People make informed decisions to mitigate the impact of food scarcity.
